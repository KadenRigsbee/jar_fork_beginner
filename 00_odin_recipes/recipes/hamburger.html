Juicest Hamburger Ever

This hamburger recipe uses milk, eggs, and bread crumbs to create incredibly juicy hamburgers.

Ingredients

  • 2lbs ground beef
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 cup bread crumbs
  • 3 tbsp evaporated milk
  • 2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/8 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

Steps

  1. Preheat grill for high heat.
  2. Mix ground beef, egg, bread crumbs, evaporated milk, Worcestershire sauce, cayenne pepper, and garlic in a large bowl using your hands.
  3. Form mixture into 8 patties.
  4. Lightly oil grill gratee. Grill patties until brown and no longer pink, about 5 minutes per side.
  5. Serve and enjoy.
