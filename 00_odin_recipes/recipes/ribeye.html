Salt and Pepper Ribeye Steak

A tender and juicy Ribeye Steak seasoned with salt and pepper.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounce ribeye steak
  • Kosher salt
  • ground black pepper
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter

Steps

  1. Remove steak from refrigerator about 30 minutes prior to cooking.
  2. Sprinkle both sides with salt and pepper.
  3. Add olive oil to cast iron pan and heat over medium-high heat until very hot.
  4. Cook steak in pan until brown and seard on one sides, 4-5 minutes.
  5. Turn steak and topw with 1 tbsp butter. cook for 3 to 4 mor minutes or to desireed doneness.
  6. Serve and enjoy.
